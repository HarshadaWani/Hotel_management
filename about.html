Welcome to Hotel Crystal  

The relaxing atmosphere of the Hotel Crystal, Bangalore offers you warm attentive service by our trained and friendly staff.

Hotel Crystal is located in the heart of the city making it conveniently close to the City's business, shopping centres and site-seeing places.

The hotel is located in close proximity to the City railway station and bus stand. The race course is a few minutes walk away and major shopping destinations such as Mantri Mall, MG Road, Commercial Street are all a short drive from the hotel.

This makes the hotel just the place to stay in, be it for a business visit or a holiday.
